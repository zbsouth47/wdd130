Trip Highlights

Half Day Trip

We run two of these trips per day. Each is approximately four hours. You'll meet us at the put in point and van will pick you up and bring you back to your vehicles. Expect some mild rapids with a couple stops along the way for pictures and meals.

Full Day Trip

We run these trips daily. You will depart early afternoon, float the river to the camping spot and spend the night. The next day you'll float to the pickup area and make several stops along the way for pictures and other exploration.

Three Day Trip

This trip only runs once per week and the whole summer gets booked fast, so make your reservation now! It takes you through several sets of rapids, some picturesque canyons and you get to spend one of the days camped by a beautiful waterfalll.
