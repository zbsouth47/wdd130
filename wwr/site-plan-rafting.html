Rafting Site Plan

Author: Zachary Southwick

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our site will be two fold. First, to educate newcomers to the hobby. We want to provide information on gear, best practices, locations, etc. Second, we want to be able to sell those things. We will have gear to sell and rent, we'll have written guides and tour guides. We'll make it a one stop shop for anyone interested in white water rafting.

Audience

Target audience will mostly be those interested in learning about the hobby.

Age demographic will be teenagers through middle ages. So making the site accessible to both groups will be important.

Financial demographic will mostly be middle and upper class. White water rafting gear is typically somewhat expensive but we want our site to still be friendly and accessible to all.

Access to the site will be geared towards tablet, laptops, desktops.

They want to access information about white water rafting: gear recommendations, guides, etc. The also want to be able to purchase gear.
